Company Overview

Ray Precicomp is a specialized manufacturer of engineered plastic pipe assemblies for the Indian automotive industry. We design and produce integrated line systems for Fuel delivery, SCR emission control, Pneumatic braking, Thermal management and Hydrogen mobility platforms.

Our solutions are engineered to meet stringent OEM standards for Permeation control, Burst resistance, Impulse fatigue, Chemical compatibility, Vibration durability and Dimensional stability.

We support full APQP lifecycle including feasibility review, DFMEA, Prototype validation, PPAP Level 3 documentation, control plans, SPC implementation and Just-In-Time serial supply aligned to OEM production schedules.
