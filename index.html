Company Overview

Ray Precicomp Pvt Ltd is a specialized manufacturer of engineered plastic pipe assemblies for the automotive industry. We develop integrated fluid transport systems for Fuel delivery, SCR emission control, Pneumatic braking, Thermal management and Hydrogen mobility platforms.

Our product architecture combines advanced polymer engineering, multilayer barrier constructions, connector integration and optimized routing geometry to deliver compact, lightweight and durable fluid routing solutions aligned with global OEM technical requirements.

We focus on complete system integration rather than individual components — reducing leak interfaces, minimizing joints and improving assembly efficiency at vehicle level.

All systems are engineered to meet stringent performance standards including: permeation control, burst resistance, impulse fatigue durability, chemical compatibility, hydrolysis resistance, thermal cycling stability and dimensional robustness under dynamic loading conditions.

We support the complete APQP lifecycle including feasibility review, DFMEA development, prototype validation, PPAP Level 3 documentation, control plans, process capability validation and Just-In-Time serial production.

Our approach integrates global system thinking with localized execution — enabling emission-compliant, cost-competitive and future-ready mobility solutions for Indian and international vehicle platforms.
