Advanced Plastic Fluid Routing Systems for Automotive, EV and Hydrogen Applications

Precision engineered plastic pipe assemblies enabling safe transport of fuel, air, SCR, coolant and hydrogen across modern vehicle platforms.

Executive Summary

Ray Precicomp specializes in precision formed plastic routing assemblies supporting fuel delivery, emission control, thermal management, pneumatic and hydrogen systems.

Our routing systems provide lightweight, corrosion-free, leak-tight and packaging-optimized solutions for passenger vehicles, commercial vehicles and future hydrogen mobility platforms.

Fluid Routing System Portfolio

Fuel Delivery Systems

Multilayer plastic fuel routing assemblies designed to safely transport gasoline, diesel and ethanol blended fuels between storage tank and engine systems.

  • Fuel feed and return lines
  • Barrier multilayer constructions
  • Integrated connectors and clips
  • Lightweight alternative to metal tubes

SCR and Emission Systems

Precision routing systems transporting urea fluid in selective catalytic reduction systems ensuring emission compliance.

  • Urea routing assemblies
  • Heated SCR systems
  • Chemical resistant construction

Thermal Management Systems

Coolant routing systems supporting engine cooling, battery cooling and electronics thermal management.

Pneumatic and Air Systems

Compressed air routing systems supporting air brake and suspension systems.

Hydrogen Routing Systems

Multilayer hydrogen compatible routing assemblies supporting hydrogen fuel cell platforms.
