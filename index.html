Company Overview

Ray Precicomp Pvt Ltd is an Next-Gen Pipe Assemblies system manufacturer specializing in high-performance plastic pipe assemblies for the automotive industry. We engineer complete line systems for Fuel delivery, SCR emission control, Pneumatic braking, Thermal management, Electrified mobility and Hydrogen platforms.

Our product architecture integrates lightweight polymer engineering, barrier-layer technology, quick connector integration and vibration-optimized routing geometry to deliver compact, high-efficiency fluid transport solutions aligned with global OEM standards.

We focus on complete system integration rather than individual components — ensuring optimized packaging, reduced joints, minimized leak interfaces and improved assembly efficiency at vehicle level.

All systems are engineered to meet stringent performance requirements including permeation control, burst resistance, impulse fatigue durability, chemical compatibility, hydrolysis resistance, thermal cycling stability and dimensional robustness under dynamic vehicle loading conditions.

We support the full APQP lifecycle including feasibility reviews, DFMEA, Prototype builds, Validation planning, PPAP Level 3 documentation, control plan development, process capability validation and Just-In-Time serial production aligned to OEM takt time.

Our approach combines global system thinking with localized execution — enabling cost-competitive, emission-compliant and future-ready fluid routing solutions for Indian and international vehicle platforms.
